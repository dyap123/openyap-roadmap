THE OPPORTUNITY

From one engineer
to industry platform.

OpenYap started at a desk inside Clune Construction. One project engineer saw the gap between what AI could do and what the industry was willing to try. When the idea was taken and rebuilt without him, he spent 18 months learning to build it himself.

Now it runs on a live Webcor project -- processing plans, tracking RFIs, managing submittals, and generating takeoffs with a purpose-built AI agent. Not a demo. Not a prototype. A working system used by a real construction team.

The pitch is simple: partner with a builder who already has the technology running, scale it across the portfolio, and own the platform that becomes the industry standard.
